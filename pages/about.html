Our Story

About Apex Moto

Family-owned and factory-certified since 1998. We believe honest work builds lasting trust.

Built on Trust, Driven by Expertise

Apex Moto started in a two-bay garage in 1998 when Mike Reyes Sr. decided San Marcos deserved an auto shop that treated every customer like family. Nearly three decades later, that philosophy hasn't changed — but our capabilities have.

Today we operate a 6-bay, fully equipped facility with ASE Master Certified technicians, factory-level diagnostic tools, and certifications for EV and hybrid platforms. We've served over 15,000 vehicles and maintained a 4.9-star rating across 1,200+ reviews.

We don't upsell. We don't cut corners. We fix cars right — and we stand behind every repair with a 3-year / 36,000-mile warranty.
